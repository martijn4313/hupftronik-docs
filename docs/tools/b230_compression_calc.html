Cam figures: measured factory-grind data (duration @1 mm lift, lift, intake centerline) via redblockpowered.com camshaft chart. IVC is derived as ICL + dur/2 − 180 at the 1 mm threshold, so dynamic-CR values are comparative, not absolute. Chamber / dish defaults are back-fitted to factory nominal CR — cc your actual parts for real numbers. Turbo flow figures are community/manufacturer ballpark values. Baro fixed at 1013 mbar. T_EOC model: ambient air is compressed to boost pressure with the given compressor isentropic efficiency (real compressor outlet temp rises with boost, not just pressure), cooled through the intercooler by its stated effectiveness, then picks up heat in-port/in-cylinder before IVC (E85 gets an evaporative credit). That trapped charge is compressed polytropically over the dynamic ratio: T·DCRn−1, P·DCRn. Still ignores combustion-phase rise, squish and hot spots — an ordering tool, in physical units, that now actually responds to boost.
